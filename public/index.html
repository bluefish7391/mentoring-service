Two Pillars, One Program

A single flexible track that meets every student where they are — from first-time coders to budding engineers.

🔭 Discovery

  • Exciting exposure to CS topics not in typical curricula
  • Spark genuine curiosity and passion for technology
  • Real-world examples: how the internet works, AI tools, and more

  • Advanced challenges and project-based progression
  • Pathway to portfolio-worthy capstone projects
